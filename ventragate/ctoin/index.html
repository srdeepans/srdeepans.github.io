About Us

As an experienced technology consulting firm, we provide access to executive-level technology expertise and on-demand five types of CTO resources on a wide range of technologies and digital platforms for established organizations and as well for start-ups. CTO services or CTO consulting is a one-of-a-kind offering in which a highly experienced technology expert, specifically selected for your business needs, collaborates with you to ease and support IT initiatives, outline tech strategies and approaches based on business goals, and manage technology infrastructure, tools, and teams. Under CTO as a service, the technology expert works as a consulting CTO with you and is available in your time zone, via your preferred communication channels, and in your office on-demand.

You will also have on-demand access to our highly specialized technology resource pool to support your projects. CTO as a service avoids long-term contracts, hiring, and infrastructure costs, instead allowing complete ownership of code and intellectual rights. CTO in a box have helped businesses all over the world solve their most complex technological problems, covering strategy and execution in a cost-effective manner.
